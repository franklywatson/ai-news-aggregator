Anthropic Launches Claude Science for Autonomous Research

Anthropic has elevated its life sciences tooling into Claude Science, a full-featured standalone agent product designed to autonomously execute biology and drug discovery research. Positioned alongside Claude Code, the agent can manage code, run compute clusters, and ensure reproducibility for scientific workflows, narrowing the gap between AI labs and specialized pharmaceutical applications.

"This signals a major shift from general-purpose LLMs toward vertical-specific agentic products that wrap complex infrastructure into autonomous workflows, potentially unlocking massive revenue streams in the pharmaceutical industry."

Multi-Teacher On-Policy Distillation Emerges as New Frontier Standard

A deep review of 2026 frontier model releases reveals that Multi-Teacher On-Policy Distillation (MOPD) is replacing monolithic reinforcement learning. Labs are now training numerous domain-specific expert models and distilling them into a single general student model to avoid capability conflicts and scale post-training organically.

"MOPD fundamentally changes how intelligence is synthesized, moving the industry away from single-model training races and towards complex, multi-agent orchestration pipelines at the training level."

Together AI Unveils Agent-Aware Inference and Discovery Frameworks

"ThunderAgent directly addresses the massive latency and compute overhead of deploying agents, proving that inference engines must be fundamentally redesigned to treat agentic loops natively rather than as isolated API calls."
