# AI News Digest

A daily briefing on what's happening in AI.

**Read the latest digest:** https://franklywatson.github.io/ai-news-aggregator/

## What's inside

Each weekday, a fresh digest covers the most significant developments across the AI landscape, organized into:

- **AI Models & Research** — new model releases, benchmarks, and notable research
- **Developer Tools & Infrastructure** — frameworks, platforms, and tooling for building with AI
- **Industry & Applications** — funding, acquisitions, product launches, and real-world deployments
- **Bluesky Highlights** — noteworthy posts from the AI community
- **AI Twitter/X Recap** — the day's most-discussed threads

Each story includes a short summary, why it matters, and a signal rating to help you focus on what's most important.

The page updates every morning, so check back daily for the latest.
